Rules on Ahasha for Seller

One account

It is allowed to create only one account on Ahasha for the seller.

Neglecting the price

It is prohibited to sell the same item on another sales channel at a lower price than on Ahasha.

Directing buyers away

Make sure the description of the product does not include logos with outside links.

Duplicate products

We recommend being attentive and check several times the product number not to duplicate the same product.

Ignoring the guidelines

We advise following guidelines in order to help us handle requests more effectively.

Product-only shot

A photo includes only a featured product and a variety of images showcasing your product from different angles without any other items.

Personal photos

It is forbidden to add personal photos to an Offer or photos not matching the product description.

Cooperation with individuals

It is forbidden to cooperate with models beyond the Ahasha platform.

Goods out of stock

You are not allowed to send offers if goods are out of stock. Make sure the item is available to send.
